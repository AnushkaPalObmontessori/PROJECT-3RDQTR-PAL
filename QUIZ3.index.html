Louis Partridge

Louis Patrick James Partridge, born in the date and year of June 3, 2003, is an English actor. He is well-known for his roles as Lord Tewkesbury in the Netflix films Enola Holmes (2020) and Enola Holmes 2 (2022), and Sid Vicious in the miniseries Pistol (2022).

He had gotten his first acting role at the year of 2013, and then appeared on the series,The Worst Witch, as a guest star. In 2017, he had gotten a lead role in Paddington 2, and in 2018, a lead role in Enola Holmes.

Louis Partridge is also a social media influencer. He had developed an interest in acting at a young age. Then, he started acting in 2014, appearing as Young Gav in About a Dog the same year. He has starred in numerous films, including Boomers, Second Skin, Amazon Adventure and Medici.
